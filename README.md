### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a passionate AI Developer and Researcher based in Turkey, specializing in **Computer Vision** and **Medical Image Analysis**. My work bridges the gap between theoretical machine learning and real-world clinical applications, ensuring AI models are robust, reliable, and unbiased.

- 🔭 **Currently researching:** Domain generalization, shortcut learning, and self-supervised learning methodologies.
